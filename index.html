About

What We Do

Scaman Collective LLC is a U.S.-based holding company formed under the laws of Wyoming and registered at 1309 Coffeen Avenue STE 1200, Sheridan, WY 82801. The company holds digital assets — including cryptocurrencies — exclusively for its own use, with all holdings maintained within United States jurisdiction.

Scaman Collective LLC operates solely for proprietary purposes and does not provide services to third parties. All digital assets are held in accounts registered to the LLC on U.S.-licensed exchanges or in self-custodial wallets owned and controlled exclusively by the company.

Disclaimer: This website is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute a service offering, business solicitation, investment advice, or financial guidance. This site is designed to provide transparency and context for due diligence and informational purposes.

Operations & Compliance

U.S.-Centric Operations

All LLC operations conform to applicable U.S. federal and state regulations. Digital assets may be acquired through licensed domestic or international payment processors but are exclusively held and managed within U.S. jurisdiction.

U.S. Exchange Custody

Assets held on U.S.-regulated exchanges are maintained in LLC-registered accounts subject to full KYB/AML verification.

Self-Custodial Wallets

Non-exchange holdings use self-custodial wallets owned exclusively by the LLC, with keys held by LLC principals in U.S. territory.

Payment Processor Compliance

Purchase transactions through international processors are executed with full identity verification, compliant with applicable U.S. BSA and FinCEN guidance.

No Third-Party Services

The LLC does not provide exchange, custody, lending, staking, or financial services to any third party. Operations are solely proprietary asset management.
